How Donations Work

Clear steps from community contributions to Mercy Corps impact.

  1. Send funds to the Donation Wallet

    Community members contribute directly to the public humanitarian address.

  2. Reach a target, then transfer to Mercy Corps

    Once thresholds are hit, the team sends funds to Mercy Corps.

  3. Publish tx hash + receipt

    We log the transaction hash and share the donation receipt publicly.

  4. Update the log and notify the community

    Records stay up to date, and the community gets clear proof.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from our community.

What is Helping Hands Coin (HHC)?

HHC is a humanitarian memecoin directing transparent donations to Mercy Corps for Gaza relief.

Is this an investment?

No. HHC focuses on charitable impact; always do your own research before engaging with any token.

How do I know the money really reaches Mercy Corps?

We publish transaction hashes, receipts, and separate wallets so anyone can verify the flow of funds.

Can I donate without buying the memecoin?

Yes. You can send crypto directly to the Donation Wallet and we will forward it to Mercy Corps.

What blockchains are supported?

We start with Ethereum and stablecoins; more chains may be added with community input.

How often will you donate?

We aim for regular transfers whenever targets are met and will announce each donation.

Will Mercy Corps officially endorse the project?

HHC is an independent community effort; we share receipts and confirmations but are not officially affiliated.

What makes this different from other memecoins?

Transparent wallets, clear donation logs, and a focus on real humanitarian outcomes.

Helping Hands Coin (HHC)
